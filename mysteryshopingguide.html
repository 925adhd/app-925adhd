ADHD Success Tips
Make this work for your brain
3 min ‚ñº

‚ö° Use Your ADHD Superpowers

  • Detail radar: You notice things others miss - perfect for evaluations
  • Novelty seeking: Each shop is a new adventure
  • Improv skills: ADHD brains are often great at thinking on their feet

üì± Essential Tools

  • Voice recorder app: Record notes immediately after leaving
  • Calendar with alerts: Never miss a deadline
  • Mileage tracker: MileIQ for tax deductions
  • Receipt photo app: Snap every receipt immediately

‚è∞ Report Writing Hack

The report is the hard part for ADHD brains. Here's the trick:

  1. Do the shop
  2. Voice-record your observations in the car
  3. Write the report SAME DAY (don't wait!)
  4. Set a 30-min timer - makes it a game
